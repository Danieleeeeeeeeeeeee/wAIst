## How wAIst Uses the Research

Every number in wAIst is calculated using:
- PUE & WUE values directly from the study  
- Region-specific carbon and water intensity  
- Server lifetime, utilization rates, and allocation assumptions  
- Sensitivity-tested formulas validated against industry benchmarks  

This results in a **clear, personal dashboard** with:
- Monthly environmental totals  
- Per-service breakdowns  
- Region-adjusted impact estimates
